My Story

While in university, I started as a Computer Science major, where I quickly learned that programming wasnâ€™t for me. I switched to Psychology after finding an interest in human interaction and behavior, but I still chose to retain a minor in Digital information systems because of my love for solving problems through technological innovation; this allowed me access to an array of informatics classes that introduced me to the world of UX/UX Design. As a result, I found that UX/UI design to be a happy marriage between my interest in human behavior and my love of problem solving technology.

My Approach

With my background in Psychology, I understand the importance of user research and personally believe that a sound design solution is always data driven. I strongly believe in the importance of understanding the needs of the user through user research as the driving force behind the a design concept. Design should first and foremost solve a need while making the experience effortless, enjoyable, and human.
